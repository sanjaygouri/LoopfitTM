LOOPFITTM IS THE FUTURE OF INDOOR TRAINING.

LoopFitTM’s balanced combination of hardware and software makes professional metrics accessible and easy to understand. LoopFitTM will fetch highly accurate data from your ride and turn your movements into tools for improvement using the inbuilt predictive and prescriptive algorithms.

How It Works

LoopFitTM brings to your indoors a perfect blend of immersive VR, IoT and analytics in a multi-player gaming environment, making your workouts more exciting and enjoyable.

While you’re at the game, it also tracks your heart rate, calories burnt, pedaling speed, power and keeps a tab on your health and fitness. Its’ hardware and virtual environment provide you the best of both – digital and real worlds.

The faster you cycle in the real world, the faster you propel in the virtual world. You could lean your body to the left or right to steer and navigate without any motion sickness.
